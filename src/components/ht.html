1991

MANIPAL HOSPITALS BANGALORE, OLD AIRPORT ROAD

The flagship hospital of Manipal Health Enterprises, Manipal Hospital HAL Airport Road, is a 600 bed quaternary care facility set up in 1991. Situated in the heart of Bangalore, Manipal Hospital HAL Airport Road provides care in over 60 specialities under one roof.

1993

MANIPAL NORTHSIDE HOSPITAL BANGALORE, MALLESHWARAM

This 83 bedded hospital caters to residents in & around Malleshwaram, and west & north Bangalore. Among other specialities, the hospital's focus departments include Intensive Care, Gynaecology, Orthopaedics and General Medicine. In addition, the hospital has an independent outpatient clinic rendering all services and health checks, OP Dialysis and Dental Care.

1994

MANIPAL HOSPITALS GOA

Manipal Hospital, Goa is one of the largest private hospitals in Goa, offering the highest number of specialities, having the highest number of full time doctors and 24/7 services of pharmacy, laboratory and paediatric and neonatal emergency. It has 6 world class operation theatres and a 235 bed facility. With highest standards of clinical expertise, nursing care and state-of-the-art hospital facilities, we provide rapid assessment, intervention and treatment for numerous common and complex conditions. Patients receive customized, individual care.

1997

MANIPAL HOSPITALS MANGALORE

A 251 bed tertiary care hospital, KMC Hospital, Mangalore located at Ambedkar Circle has an entire range of sub-specialities and high end diagnostic capabilities. The hospital enjoys a close association with the academic faculty members of Manipal University. The laboratory at KMC Hospital is the first in the region to be accredited by the National Accreditation Board for Testing and Calibration Laboratories (NABL) and so is its blood bank (NABL for Blood Banks). A major expansion plan is currently under implementation which will add another 158 beds to the facility.

2006

MANIPAL HOSPITALS VIJAYAWADA

Manipal Hospital, Vijayawada is one of the largest hospitals in coastal Andhra Pradesh. This tertiary care hospital has a capacity of 350 beds and high end diagnostic facilities. It currently has 240 beds operational. The hospital is renowned for its comprehensive tertiary care specialties.

2007

MANIPAL HOSPITALS SALEM

Manipal Hospital, Salem is a 110 bed tertiary care hospital located on the Salem-Bangalore expressway. By hint of its location, it offers easy accessibility to the adjoining districts of Tamil Nadu. The hospital is one of the most comprehensive tertiary care hospital with capabilities in Cardiac Care, Neuro Surgery, Gastroenterology, etc.

2008

DR.MALATHI MANIPAL HOSPITAL BANGALORE JAYANAGAR

Located in South Bangalore, the 70 beds Dr. Malathi Manipal Hospital is a secondary care facility that offers convenient healthcare for people residing in neighbourhood. The hospital's focus departments include Mother & Child Care, Intensive Care & General Surgery among others.

2014

MANIPAL HOSPITALS JAIPUR

The focus at Manipal Hospital is to develop an affordable tertiary care multispecialty healthcare framework through its entire delivery spectrum and further extend it to homecare.

2016

MANIPAL HOSPITALS WHITEFIELD

Manipal Hospital Whitefield (MHW) is a unit of the renowned Manipal Hospitals network that is known for Quality and Patient care. It is a 280 beds multi-specialty Hospital offering world class services in the Whitefield area of Bangalore city. Manipal Hospital Whitefield covers the complete range of Preventive and Curative care.

2018

MANIPAL HOSPITALS DELHI

Manipal Hospital Dwarka, is a multi super speciality tertiary care healthcare facility, delivering world-class healthcare, at an optimum cost. Our aim is to deliver Affordable, Accessible and Accurate healthcare to all sections of the society, without bias.

2021

COLUMBIA ASIA HOSPITALS

Columbia Asia Hospitals joins the Manipal Hospitals family, making it India's second-largest healthcare network. Columbia Asia is an international healthcare group operating a chain of modern hospitals across Asia. Columbia Asia Hospitals Pvt. Ltd. is one of the first healthcare companies to enter India through a 100% foreign direct investment route. The first hospital in India commenced operations in 2005 in Hebbal – Bangalore and currently, Columbia Asia operates eleven facilities. The group has a presence in Bangalore, Mysore, Kolkata, Gurugram, Ghaziabad, Patiala, and Pune.
